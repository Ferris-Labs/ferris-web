{{< faq question="Describe the approach regarding updates/upgrades and explain any service break times.Is a rolling upgrade possible?" >}}

<!-- ANSWER -->

With regard to updates and upgrades, the platform offers support for so -called rolling upgrades.This approach enables the platform components to be updated without interrupting the service.The platform remains functional during the rolling upgrade, since the update is gradually carried out on different parts of the cluster.
